🔵 QR Code Generator – About Us

📌 What is a QR Code?

QR Code (Quick Response Code) is a 2D barcode used to store:

  • Website URLs
  • Text
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • WiFi credentials
  • vCards (contact info)

Scan with any phone camera to access info instantly — no typing needed!

🎯 What Our Tool Does

Quickly generate clean, scannable QR codes for various use cases like sharing social media, contact cards, and websites.

  • 💡 Easy-to-use interface
  • ⚡ Instant generation
  • 🎨 Supports many data types
  • 📱 Works on all devices
  • 🔐 No data stored – 100% privacy
  • 🖨️ Print-ready codes
